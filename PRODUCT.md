# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Individual business owners and tech startups. They are looking for a high-performing development duo who combines creative flair with technical precision. They value trust, clarity, and a "warm minimalist" aesthetic that feels human and bespoke, not mass-produced or generic.

## Product Purpose
To showcase the combined freelance expertise of **Rythamo Studio** (Vishnu aka Rythamo & Rahul) and their previous projects in a way that converts visitors into leads. The page must demonstrate high craft, technical competence, and a unique shared creative perspective.

## Brand Personality
Creative, Technical, Precise, Warm, Minimalist.

## Anti-references
- AI-generated slop (generic cards, overused gradients, template-y layouts).
- SaaS-cliché "hero-metric" blocks.
- Over-designed "glassmorphism" that lacks purpose.

## Design Principles
- **Human Precision**: Combine technical sharpness (grid-based, precise typography) with "warm" touches (subtle organic textures, warm-tinted neutrals) to avoid an "AI-generated" look.
- **Craft as Proof**: Every interaction and layout choice must demonstrate intentionality and high craft.
- **Minimalist Clarity**: Only what matters. No filler content or restated headings.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA standard.
- High contrast typography for readability.
- Support for reduced motion.
